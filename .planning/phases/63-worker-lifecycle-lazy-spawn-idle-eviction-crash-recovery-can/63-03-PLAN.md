<objective>
Add the crash-recovery respawn-storm guard (D-06 / WLIFE-03): track consecutive early-exit/boot failures per (model×prompt) key, and once a key crosses `LLM_PROXY_WORKER_CRASH_THRESHOLD` crashes within `LLM_PROXY_WORKER_CRASH_WINDOW_MS`, put the key in "cooldown" — route its requests straight to the existing execFile overflow (`completeClaudeCodeViaCLI`) for the cooldown duration instead of spawn→crash→RETRYABLE on every request. After the window elapses, the key may lazily spawn again.

Purpose: There is already NO auto-restart (respawn is lazy-on-next-request), so WLIFE-03's "never auto-restarted in a tight loop" is structurally true. The cooldown additionally protects against a PERSISTENTLY-broken key (bad OAuth/flags) slow-failing or spin-looping every request — it degrades that key to today's pre-milestone execFile behavior rather than a crash storm (the structural answer to ROADMAP SC-3).
Output: per-key crash-frequency tracking, a cooldown gate at the top of `complete()`, two env knobs, a boot-crash mock helper, and unit coverage.
</objective>
