<objective>
Build the OpenCode read-only SQLite route reader and the `buildNormalizedTrace` dispatcher that picks a reader by the run's dominant agent, scopes RouteEvents to the span time-window (Pitfall 7 — the single biggest correctness risk), and returns `null` (not `[]`) when no trace is found (D-02 honest fallback).

Purpose: OpenCode stores everything in `opencode.db` (no JSONL) — the `part` table `type:'tool'` rows carry full per-tool fidelity. The dispatcher unifies all three readers (Claude/Copilot from Plan 03 + OpenCode here) behind one entry point the close orchestrator (Plan 05) calls.

Output: `lib/lsl/route/opencode-route-trace.mjs`, `lib/lsl/route/build-trace.mjs`, and a node:test suite for the reader + dispatcher.
</objective>
