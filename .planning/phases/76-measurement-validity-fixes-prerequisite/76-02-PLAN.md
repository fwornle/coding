<objective>
Redefine `wallclockPerStep` (`lib/experiments/route-heuristics.mjs §82-95`) so route time is the sum of *active* inter-event gaps ÷ step count, excluding operator-thinking / AFK pauses (D-05). The naïve `(lastTerminal − firstStart) / count` divides an entire multi-hour interactive window (with long AskUserQuestion steering pauses) by a handful of steps, producing the ~28,000 s/step artifact O2 exposed. Excluding gaps longer than a documented idle threshold (D-06) removes the pauses; no separate per-step outlier cap is needed.

Purpose: VALID-02 — a multi-hour session with steering pauses must yield per-step times within a documented sane bound. The metric stays deterministic and pure — no LLM (D-07).
Output: A corrected pure `wallclockPerStep` plus a named idle constant with an env override, and true-negative/edge-case tests.
</objective>
