## The core finding

v7.5 was scoped as **Phases 76–80** delivering 14 requirements. The actual *experiment-runner product* — "declare a variant matrix, run each variant autonomously from an identical snapshot, gate on success, aggregate variance, and present a ranked side-by-side comparison" — lives in phases **78 (runner), 79 (comparison), 80 (surface + skill)**.

What happened instead: after phase 77, work pivoted to a large set of **inserted infrastructure phases (82–87)** — proxy wire-measurement, token reconciliation, per-turn context, the experiment control center, timeline v2, and interactive-span/avenue forking. These are real, substantial, and mostly verified — but they are **not the original 76–80 requirements**, and they were completed *around* the unfinished runner core rather than closing it.

Net result: the **comparison + surface half of the product does not exist**, and the runner half is built-and-exercised but never formally verified through its own gate.
